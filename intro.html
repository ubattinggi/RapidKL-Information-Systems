What is an Information System?
Short: people + technology + processes that turn data into useful information.
‚Ä∫

Information System (IS) is a socio-technical system that combines information technology, people, and organizational processes to collect, process, store, and distribute information. It is not limited to just computers or software but also includes the human and social aspects involved in creating, sharing, and using information to support decision-making and improve organizational performance.
