Enterprise System
How RapidKL manages commuter data

Customer Relationship Management (CRM)

RapidKL‚Äôs MyRapid PULSE app serves as a CRM: it collects commuter profiles, travel patterns, feedback, card usage and interactions. Centralising this data helps personalise communications, resolve issues faster, and keep records up to date.

Oracle Database (back-end)

The CRM is backed by an Oracle database, an enterprise-grade DBMS. Oracle delivers ACID-compliant transaction processing (so every card tap or ticket sale is recorded reliably), scalable performance for peak loads, and strong security to protect passenger data.

Oracle also integrates with AFC, ERP and HRIS, forming a unified data backbone for analytics and operations.

In short:

  • CRM = front-end commuter management and communication.
  • Oracle DB = secure back-end storage, transaction safety, scalability, and cross-system integration.
