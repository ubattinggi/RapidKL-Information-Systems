Operations
Scheduling & coordination

Operations uses RapidKL CRM data to manage routes and schedules. The CRM combines information from card taps, the MyRapid PULSE app and commuter feedback, helping operations teams see crowding patterns, common delays and peak periods so they can adjust frequency, dispatch extra trains and plan maintenance at low-impact times.

‚öôÔ∏è Operational Level

CRM gives front-line staff a real-time view of crowd levels, delays, complaints and service updates. This helps them make quick decisions such as redirecting passengers, updating announcements and reallocating vehicles to keep daily operations running smoothly.

üß© Managerial Level

Managers use CRM dashboards and trend reports to analyse ridership, recurring issues and station performance. These insights guide decisions on timetable adjustments, staff deployment and service improvements for specific routes or locations.

üöÄ Strategic Level

At the strategic level, executives rely on CRM analytics to understand long-term commuter behaviour. CRM data supports decisions about new service offerings, digital features in the app, pricing strategies and partnerships that can strengthen RapidKL competitive position.
